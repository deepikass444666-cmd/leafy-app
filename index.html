Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: April 2025

Data We Collect

Leafy collects your email address and plant care data to deliver reminders. We do not sell your data to third parties. All data is stored securely in MongoDB and processed via AWS Lambda.

Email Reminders

By registering, you consent to receive plant care reminder emails via AWS SES. You can unsubscribe at any time from the Settings page.

Local Storage

Plant data is stored in your browser's local storage for offline access. Clearing your browser data will remove locally stored plants.

Terms of Service

Leafy is provided as-is for personal plant care purposes. We are not responsible for any plant-related outcomes. Please water your plants responsibly.
