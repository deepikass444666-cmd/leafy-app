🌿 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

What we collect

Leafy stores your plant data locally in your browser. If you enable email reminders, we collect only your email address to send care notifications. AI features process images temporarily and do not store them.

How we use it

Your data is used exclusively to provide plant care reminders and AI-powered insights. We never sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

AI & Images

Photos uploaded to AI Tools are sent to our AI provider for analysis and are not retained after processing. No images are stored on our servers.
